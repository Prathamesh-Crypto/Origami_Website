Interesting facts on camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting facts on chameleon

  • Chameleon are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleon.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensive tail that use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts on cicada

  • Cicadas can't eat, getting nutrients from tree roots.
  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.
  • They have one of the longest insect life spans.

Interesting facts on pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigational abilites.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilites.

Interesting facts on chameleon

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
